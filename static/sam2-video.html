SAM2 Video — client-side object tracking

Load a video, scrub to a starting frame, then left-click the object to segment it (right-click/Shift+click adds background points). Press Track to follow the object through the clip and record an annotated video. Everything runs in your browser via SAM2.
Note: this uses per-frame SAM2 segmentation with centroid propagation — the browser ONNX export has no temporal-memory module, so fast motion or occlusion may lose the object.
